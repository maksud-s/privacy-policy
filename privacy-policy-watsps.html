Privacy Policy

Effective date: Jun 08, 2026

Status Saver is provided by Khushi Digital Solutions as a free mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is accessed, used, stored, and shared when you use the app.

1. App Independence

Status Saver is an independent utility application. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to any external messaging platform, social platform, company, product, brand, or device manufacturer. All names, logos, trademarks, and media shown by external services remain the property of their respective owners.

2. Information We Access

The app is designed to work mostly on your device. Depending on the feature you choose to use, the app may access the following information:

  • Media files and folders that you select or allow, so the app can display, preview, save, repost, share, or manage supported status media files.
  • Photos, videos, storage, or folder access permission where required by Android, so the app can read supported media files and save selected files to your device.
  • Phone number, country code, and optional message text entered in the Direct Chat feature. This information is used only to open the selected chat flow through the external app chosen by the user.
  • Basic app settings stored locally on your device, such as theme preference, selected folders, favorites, collections, update prompts, and ad display settings.
  • Advertising identifiers, device information, approximate network information, diagnostics, and ad interaction data may be processed by advertising SDKs used in the app.

3. Current Android Permissions

The current release of the app uses only the permissions needed for the features available in the app:

  • Internet and network state: used to load web-based features, check app update configuration, show advertisements, and detect basic network availability.
  • Storage access on Android 9 and below only: read/write storage permissions are declared with Android's maxSdkVersion 28 limit. On newer Android versions, the app uses Android folder/media access flows instead of broad storage permission.
  • Advertising ID: used by Google advertising services for ad delivery, measurement, frequency control, fraud prevention, and related advertising functions.
  • Wake lock: this permission may be included by Google/advertising services in the final app package to keep required SDK operations reliable. The app does not use it to collect personal content.

The app does not request or use microphone, camera, location, contacts, call log, SMS, notification, foreground service, or audio recording permissions.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Khushi Digital Solutions does not operate a user account system for this app and does not collect or store your personal media files, saved status files, phone numbers, contact list, direct message text, or gallery content on our own servers.

5. How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the app is used only for app functionality, including:

  • showing and previewing supported media files available on your device;
  • saving selected photos and videos to your device;
  • sharing or reposting files only when you choose that action;
  • keeping favorites and collections locally on your device;
  • opening a direct chat flow when you enter a number/message and tap send;
  • checking remote configuration for app updates and ad display settings;
  • serving, measuring, limiting, and protecting advertisements.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal content. We do not upload your media files, saved files, phone numbers, or message text to our own servers. Information may be shared only in these situations:

  • When you choose to share, repost, save, or open content through another app or external service.
  • When you choose a backup or share option, Android's share sheet may be used so you can send selected saved files to another app or service you choose. The app does not access your external accounts or upload files by itself.
  • Advertising partners may receive device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data as needed to show ads, prevent fraud, measure performance, and comply with applicable laws.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or the public.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party SDKs and services for advertising, app updates, remote configuration, ad-related measurement, fraud prevention, and app operation. These providers may process information under their own privacy policies.

  • Google AdMob and Google services: Google Privacy Policy, Google Advertising, and Google Partner Sites.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Khushi Digital Solutions does not retain your personal media, chat input, or selected files on our own servers. Files saved through the app remain on your device until you delete them. You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app's storage from Android settings, deleting saved files from your device, or revoking folder/media permissions from Android settings.

9. Security

The app uses Android's permission system and local device storage controls to help protect your information. Because no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we limit access to what is needed for the features you use.

10. Children's Privacy

Status Saver is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the concern.
