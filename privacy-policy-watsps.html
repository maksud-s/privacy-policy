Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

Status Saver is provided by The Mystery Lens as a free mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is accessed, used, stored, and shared when you use the app.

1. App Independence

Status Saver is an independent utility. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to any external messaging platform, social platform, or device manufacturer. All names, logos, and media shown by external services remain the property of their respective owners.

2. Information We Access

The app is designed to work mostly on your device. Depending on the feature you choose to use, the app may access the following information:

  • Media files and folders that you select or allow, so the app can display, preview, save, repost, or share temporary media/status files.
  • Photos and videos permission, or storage/folder permission, so the app can read supported media files and save selected files to your device.
  • Phone number, country code, and message text entered in the Direct Chat feature. This information is used only to open the selected conversation flow through the external chat service chosen by the user.
  • Camera, microphone, file, and notification permissions where required by Android or by a web-based companion feature. These permissions are requested only when the related feature needs them.
  • Basic app settings stored on your device, such as theme preference, selected folders, update prompts, and ad display settings.
  • Advertising identifiers, device information, approximate network information, diagnostics, and ad interaction data may be processed by advertising SDKs used in the app.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The Mystery Lens does not operate a user account system for this app and does not collect or store your personal media files, saved status files, phone numbers, contact list, direct message text, or gallery content on our own servers.

4. How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the app is used only for app functionality, including:

  • showing and previewing media files available on your device;
  • saving selected files to your gallery or downloads folder;
  • sharing or reposting files only when you choose that action;
  • opening a direct chat flow when you enter a number/message and tap send;
  • checking a remote configuration file for app update and ad display settings;
  • serving, measuring, limiting, and protecting advertisements.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal content. We do not upload your media files, saved files, phone numbers, or message text to our own servers. Information may be shared only in these situations:

  • When you choose to share, repost, save, or open content through another app or external service.
  • Advertising partners may receive device identifiers, ad identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data as needed to show ads, prevent fraud, measure performance, and comply with law.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or the public.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party SDKs and services for advertising, mediation, update configuration, and app operation. These providers may process information under their own privacy policies.

  • Google AdMob and Google services: Google Privacy Policy, Google Advertising, and Google Partner Sites.
  • Unity LevelPlay / ironSource and related ad mediation services: Unity Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

The Mystery Lens does not retain your personal media, chat input, or selected files on our own servers. Files saved through the app remain on your device until you delete them. You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app's storage from Android settings, deleting saved files from your device, or revoking folder/media permissions from Android settings.

8. Security

The app uses Android's permission system and local device storage controls to help protect your information. Because no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we limit access to what is needed for the features you use.

9. Children's Privacy

Status Saver is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the concern.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
