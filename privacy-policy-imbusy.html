Privacy Policy

I Am Busy is developed by The Mystery Lens and provided as a free utility app.

1. Information We Collect

The Busy Mode app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All features operate strictly on-device, ensuring full privacy.

2. Permissions Used

This app requests the following permissions:

  • Read Phone State – to detect incoming calls
  • Answer Phone Calls – to simulate rejecting a call
  • Call Phone – to manage call states if necessary

These permissions are used only for functionality related to the Busy Mode and not for collecting any user information.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob for displaying ads. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data to improve ad targeting. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Busy Mode app is not intended for children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
