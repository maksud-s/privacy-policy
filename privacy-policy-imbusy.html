Privacy Policy - I Am Busy Now

This Privacy Policy describes how I Am Busy Now (the "App"), developed by The Mystery Lens, collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Last Updated: July 09, 2025

1. Information We Collect and Process

Our App is designed to provide call management functionality. To deliver this service, we access certain device information and capabilities:

1.1 Device Information We Access

  • Phone State Information: We access your device's phone state to detect incoming calls and manage call responses. This includes call status (ringing, idle, in-call) but does not include call content, call logs, or call recordings.
  • Network Information: We access network connectivity status to ensure proper app functionality and ad delivery.
  • Device Identifiers: Through Google AdMob, we may collect advertising identifiers and device information for ad personalization and analytics.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal contact information (names, phone numbers, email addresses)
  • Call logs or call history
  • Call recordings or call content
  • SMS or messaging data
  • Location data
  • Personal photos, files, or documents

2. How We Use Information

The information we access is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Core App Functionality: To detect incoming calls and automatically respond with a busy signal when the app is activated
  • Service Operation: To run the foreground service that monitors phone state and manages call responses
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • App Performance: To ensure network connectivity for proper app operation
  • Notifications: To display service status notifications when the app is running

3. Permissions Explained

Our App requests the following permissions, which are used only for the stated purposes:

  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Required to detect incoming calls and current phone status
  • ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS: Required to allow the app (when set as default dialer) to detect and reject incoming calls by simulating a busy status
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to run the call monitoring service in the background
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_PHONE_CALL: Required for Android 14+ to run phone call related foreground services
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to display service status notifications
  • INTERNET: Required for displaying advertisements and checking app updates
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to check network connectivity before internet operations
Important: We only access phone state information necessary for call management. We do not record, store, or transmit your calls or personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information according to Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • App usage analytics
  • Ad interaction data

For complete details on Google's data practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4.1 Advertising Controls

You can control ad personalization by:

  • Visiting your device's ad settings (Android Settings > Google > Ads)
  • Opting out of personalized advertising
  • Resetting your advertising ID

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not store personal information, call logs, or sensitive data on our servers
  • Local Processing: All call management functions are processed locally on your device
  • Secure Transmission: Any data transmitted to third parties (like AdMob) uses secure protocols
  • Limited Data Access: We only access the minimum data necessary for app functionality

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Google AdMob: For advertising and analytics purposes, as described in their privacy policy
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. User Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Permission Control: You can revoke app permissions through your device settings
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • App Uninstall: You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data processing
  • Data Requests: Contact us for questions about data practices

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers
